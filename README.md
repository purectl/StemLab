<h2 align="center">
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/🚀_QUICK_OVERVIEW-44C6CA?style=for-the-badge" alt="Quick Overview">
</h2>

<p align="center">
  StemLab is a powerful, free, and open-source tool that uses state-of-the-art AI models to separate any audio file into individual stems (vocals, drums, bass, and other instruments). Whether you're a musician, producer, DJ, or content creator, StemLab gives you professional-quality stem separation without the expensive price tag.
</p>

<p align="center">
  <b>All processing happens locally on your machine</b> — your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy.
</p>
